Overview

Purpose

This website's purpose is to help facilitate local tourists and adventurerers who want to go water rafting. This website has pricing, information on packages, routes and contact info.

Audience

Who are the target customer? Decribe their life situation (age, financial situation, interest, and so on)

  • Most likely in their mid 20s to mid 40s, middle class to upper-middle class, nature-loving and adventurous.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

  • They expect to find pricing, perhaps photos and reviews from other fellow water rafters. They are looking and comparing not only my rates, but also the quality of experience that I deliver, versus my competitors.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

  • Through mobile device while traveling and needing last minute information/pricing before booking.
